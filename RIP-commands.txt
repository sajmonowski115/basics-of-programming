RIP

*Dodawanie sieci w RIP
# conf t
(config)# router rip
(config-router)# network ADDRESS/MASK
np. network 192.168.1.0/24

*Ustanawianie sąsiedztwa pomiędzy dwoma ruterami RIP
# conf t
(config)# router rip
(config-router)# neighbor ADDRESS
np. neighbor 192.168.3.2

# show ip rip
